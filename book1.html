"Ratan Tata – A Life" by Thomas Mathew IAS is a well-written and insightful book about one of India’s most admired leaders. It shares fascinating details about his life, including many lesser-known facts. The author has put in great effort to research and present Tata’s journey as a businessman, visionary, and philanthropist. The book is engaging and inspiring, making it a great read for anyone interested in leadership, entrepreneurship, or the Tata legacy.
